Spotlight on Health with CNS Webinar

Childhood Obesity: Not Just in Their Genes

Wednesday, March 8th, 2023 @ 7:00 pm E.T.

Childhood obesity is an ever-growing problem in our country. According to the CDC, 1 in 5 children is considered obese. As a result, we are seeing the number of chronic illnesses increase exponentially in our children, including diabetes, high cholesterol and high blood pressure. But what can we do to fight back against childhood obesity? Come join us as we explore the timely issue of childhood obesity, focusing on its causes, its effects, and how we can help combat childhood obesity with a unique collaborative approach.
